ABOUT THE APP

Easy And Perfect Solution For Your
Travel Experience

FordaCOMMUTE is an application where users can see the routes and terminals of different kinds of transportation modes such as tricycles, jeeps, vans, and buses. It will also display the schedule when drivers begin driving and their last trips of the day. In addition to this, the app also tells the user where the usual pick-up/drop-off areas of different vehicles.
